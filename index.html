ivelum is a distributed team
of engineers headquartered in Vilnius,  Lithuania. We've been helping start-ups and established businesses build their products since 2003. We also have our own projects, like Teamplify.

Almost 3 million users have registered with our products. And the number is growing.

Services

We specialize in full-stack web development. Our work builds both rapid
idea prototypes and complete, production-ready systems scaling to millions of users. We cover all angles ─ problem analysis, backend, frontend, UX, and operations ─ all while providing project management each step of the way.

We emphasize creativity and optimize for efficiency. We like to experiment and learn, but our teams stay focused on their goals to deliver fast results in short iteration cycles. Our philosophy is move fast and don't break things.

Technology

Python, Django,and Celery are
our favorite tools for the backend. On
the frontend, we rely on ES2019, React.js, and SASS. We choose either GraphQL or REST for our APIs. Most of our projects are hosted on AWS, a platform we're deeply familiar with. For our data manipulation tasks, Redshift, Google BigQuery, Spark, Pandas, and a bunch of other technologies have worked well.

Our release cycle is fast and heavily automated. We use Docker with orchestration and automated infrastructure management with Terraform or CloudFormation. We also use CI and CD for all of our projects and release to production frequently: often dozens of times per day. Datadog, New Relic, Sentry, and CloudWatch help
us monitor the production systems' health.

We love open-source!

Our developers have contributed to
to Django, stylelint, django-redis and many other open-source projects. We built and maintain DjangoQL, an advanced search language for Django.

Teamplify

Teamplify is a personal assistant for software development teams. It connects to your existing tools, like Jira, Git, Slack, or Zoom, and helps visualize work progress and spot situations that may require the attention of a manager or team lead. Though we initially created Teamplify as an internal tool, it's worked so well for us that we thought other teams might benefit from it too. Learn more
